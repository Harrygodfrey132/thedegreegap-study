How to use past papers effectively

01

Find your board and year

Select your exam board above, then pick the year and series. June papers are the main sitting; November papers are available for some boards.

02

Work in timed conditions

90 minutes per paper under exam rules gives the most realistic practice. Sit it without notes or hints first.

03

Self-mark using the mark scheme

Pay close attention to method marks — partial credit is awarded for correct working even when the final answer is wrong.

04

Close the gaps

Identify which topics you dropped marks on. A specialist tutor can help you close those gaps faster than working through them alone.
