Edexcel GCSE Maths (by Pearson) is one of the most popular GCSE Maths specifications in England and Wales. The exam consists of three papers: Paper 1 is non-calculator, and Papers 2 and 3 are calculator papers. Foundation tier covers grades 1–5 and Higher tier covers grades 4–9.
