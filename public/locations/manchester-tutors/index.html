Find the Right Tutor in Manchester

Manchester is one of the largest cities in the country, with a secondary school landscape to match. Across its comprehensive schools, teachers work hard in large classes — but there is rarely time to slow down and explain something a different way for a student who is not quite keeping up. That pull towards selective education affects families across Greater Manchester, and for students in the city's comprehensives who are struggling quietly, it can sharpen the pressure.

The Degree Gap works with Manchester students who are capable but not yet showing it. Our tutors begin by finding the exact point where things stopped making sense — not a recap of a lesson already half-understood, but a proper look at what has gone wrong and why. From there, sessions are practical and structured, building the understanding that leads to better results. Whether a student needs a GCSE tutor who can explain things clearly without the pace of a classroom, or an A-Level tutor for a subject that has never quite clicked, one-to-one tuition gives them the focused support they deserve.

Experienced Tutors

Personalised Learning

Online tuition that fits around Manchester students' lives

All tuition with Manchester students is delivered online through Lessonspace, a platform designed for focused one-to-one sessions. Students and tutors work together on a shared interactive whiteboard, which is particularly useful for Maths and the sciences where working through problems step by step matters as much as knowing the answer. There is no need to travel across a large city, making it much easier to keep sessions consistent throughout the term.

Consistency is what makes the real difference. A student who meets their tutor each week across a half term will almost always make more progress than one who books several sessions in the week before an exam. Manchester families tell us that the online format makes regular sessions far easier to commit to — no travel, no scheduling around traffic, and the flexibility to book at a time that works around school and other activities. For students rebuilding confidence, that steady weekly rhythm is often exactly what turns things around.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

My child is struggling with confidence. Can you help?

Absolutely. Many of the students we work with across Manchester are not struggling because of ability. They have lost confidence after a difficult topic or a run of poor results, and our tutors are experienced at rebuilding that steadily and patiently.

How are sessions structured in Manchester?

Each session is tailored to the individual student. Tutors typically review recent school work, tackle problem areas directly and use past paper questions to build exam confidence. There is no one-size-fits-all approach for Manchester students.

Is one-to-one tutoring better than group sessions?

For most students, yes. One-to-one sessions mean the tutor focuses entirely on one student's gaps, questions and pace, with no waiting for others to catch up. It is particularly valuable for Manchester students who are hesitant to ask questions in a group setting.

How quickly can tuition start in Manchester?

Once we understand the subject, level and goals, we aim to make a suitable introduction quickly. Most families in Manchester hear back within a few days, depending on tutor availability.
