Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Get to know The Degree Gap?

The Degree Gap is a tutoring company founded by Oxford and LSE graduates. We provide one-to-one GCSE and A-Level tutoring across South London and Surrey, with a focus on academic results and student confidence.

Who are your tutors

All our tutors are graduates from the UK's most prestigious universities — including Oxford, Cambridge, LSE, Imperial, and UCL. Every tutor is DBS-checked, personally interviewed, and trained before their first session.

What subjects do you cover?

We cover all major GCSE and A-Level subjects including Maths, English, Science (Biology, Chemistry, Physics), History, Geography, Economics, Psychology, Spanish, French, and more. We also offer 11+ preparation and university personal statement tutoring.

How does payment work?

Sessions are booked and paid for through our secure platform. We offer flexible packages and there is no long-term commitment. You can book individual sessions or bundles at a discounted rate.

Do you tutor outside of the UK?

Yes. All our sessions are available online via video call, so we work with students anywhere in the world. We have supported students across Europe, the Middle East, and Asia who are following UK curricula.
