Experienced GCSE and A-Level Tutors in Southampton

King Edward VI School sits at the top of Southampton's secondary rankings, year after year. Selective, independent, consistently leading the region on grades 9 to 7. Most students in the city don't go there. They attend one of Southampton's state comprehensives, where classes are large and the curriculum moves fast. And for many of them, Year 10 is where things start to unravel. Not because they are not capable. Because gaps from Year 8 or 9, things that seemed minor at the time, are now getting in the way of harder content.

That is exactly what one-to-one tuition is built for. Our Southampton tutors do not open with this week's lesson topic. They start further back. What does the student actually understand? Where did things stop making sense? Once that is clear, sessions are built around closing those specific gaps, not repeating content that has already been half-taught and half-understood. For a student whose grades are not reflecting their ability, that kind of targeted approach is often what actually shifts things.

High Quality Standards

Personalised Learning

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Can you support students who have fallen significantly behind?

Yes. We regularly work with Southampton students who have gaps stretching back a year or more. The first step is finding exactly where those gaps are. Then we build a structured plan to close them at a pace that actually works. There is no point covering new content if the foundations underneath it are shaky.

How long does it take to see an improvement?

Most Southampton students notice a difference within the first few sessions. Not necessarily in grades straight away, but in how the subject feels and how much less daunting the harder questions seem. Grade improvements typically follow within a half term of consistent sessions.

How much does tutoring cost in Southampton?

Sessions start from £37 per hour. The final rate depends on the subject and tutor experience, agreed directly with your tutor before anything is committed to. No hidden fees, no booking charges.

Do you offer tutoring during school holidays in Southampton?

Yes. Holiday sessions are popular with Southampton families who want to get ahead before a new term, catch up after a difficult one, or work intensively before upcoming exams. We arrange sessions around whatever the family needs.
