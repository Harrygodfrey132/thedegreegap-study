Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Do you help with sixth form entrance exams or interviews in Hitchin?

Yes. We work with Hitchin students preparing for competitive sixth form entry, including the grade requirements for Hitchin Girls' sixth form and aptitude tests or interviews at other schools. Tutors know what these transitions require and prepare students on content and exam performance, not just one or the other.

Can tutors help with GCSE mock preparation in Hitchin?

Yes. Mocks are one of the most common reasons families come to us. Tutors work through past papers, tighten exam technique and focus on the topics most likely to come up. Students go in knowing what to expect.

Is one-to-one tutoring better than group sessions?

For most students, yes. The whole session is about them. Their gaps, their questions, their pace. No waiting for others to catch up. No pressure to keep up with the rest. For students in Hitchin who struggle to ask questions in front of others, it is often the thing that changes everything.

How long does it take to see an improvement?

Most students notice something within the first few sessions. Not always in grades straight away, but in how the subject feels. Less daunting. Clearer. Grade improvements usually follow within a half term of regular sessions.
