Tutors Who Know What Leicester Students Need

Across Leicester, we work with students who are clearly capable but whose results keep falling short. They engage in lessons, they put the effort in at home, and they genuinely care about doing well. But something keeps getting in the way. In a city where secondary schools serve large, diverse cohorts, classroom teachers rarely have the time to find exactly where an individual student is losing marks. The gap between ability and outcome can sit there for months before anyone pins down the cause, and that is where a tutor makes the difference.

One-to-one tuition is where that changes. The Degree Gap matches Leicester students with tutors who can sit with them, go through their work carefully, and find the specific point where understanding breaks down. It might be exam technique. It might be a topic from a previous year that was never properly resolved. It might simply be that a student needs someone to explain things in a different way, without the pace of a busy classroom. Whatever the cause, the right tutor makes a real difference to what shows up in results.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I find the right tutor in Leicester?

Start by telling us the subject, the student's year group and their goals. We work with families across Leicester and can usually suggest someone suitable quickly once we understand what the student needs. You are not left searching through profiles yourself.

Can you support students who have fallen significantly behind?

Yes. We regularly work with Leicester students who have gaps stretching back a year or more. The first step is identifying exactly where the gaps are, then building a structured plan to close them at a pace that works for the student.

My child is struggling with confidence. Can you help?

Absolutely. Many of the students we work with across Leicester are not struggling because of ability. They have lost confidence after a difficult topic or a run of poor results, and our tutors are experienced at rebuilding that steadily and patiently.

How long does it take to see an improvement?

Most Leicester students notice a difference within the first few sessions, not necessarily in grades straight away, but in how they approach the subject and how much less daunting it feels. Grade improvements typically follow within a half term of consistent sessions.
