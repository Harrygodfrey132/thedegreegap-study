AQA GCSE Maths is the most widely-sat GCSE specification in England. The exam consists of three papers: Paper 1 is non-calculator, while Papers 2 and 3 are calculator papers. Foundation tier targets grades 1–5 and Higher tier targets grades 4–9. All papers are sat at the end of Year 11.

Use the papers below to practise exam technique, identify topic gaps, and build confidence ahead of your exams. Each page includes the full question paper and mark scheme.
