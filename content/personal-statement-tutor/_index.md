A strong personal statement should make the reader feel that the student has found a genuine academic thread. It should not sound like a list of achievements, a summary of books, or a polished version of something generic.

The Degree Gap works subject by subject because the expectations are different. Medicine needs judgement, reflection, ethics, and care. Economics needs analysis and evidence. Computer Science needs abstraction and projects. English Literature needs close reading and argument. Our job is to help students find the version of their story that belongs to the course they are applying for.
