Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is The Degree Gap?

The Degree Gap is a tutoring company founded by Oxford and LSE graduates. We connect families across South London and Surrey with experienced, independent GCSE and A-Level tutors who are hand-picked for their subject knowledge, teaching ability, and professionalism.

What subjects do the tutors in your network cover?

Our tutors cover all major GCSE and A-Level subjects including Maths, English Language and Literature, Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Economics, History, Geography, Psychology, Spanish, French, and more. We also support students with personal statement writing and 11+ preparation.

What exam boards do the tutors cover?

The tutors in our network are experienced across all the major UK exam boards, including AQA, Edexcel, OCR, WJEC, and Cambridge International (IGCSE and A-Level). When we match your child with a tutor, we make sure they know the specific board and syllabus inside out.

Who are the tutors in your network?

All our tutors are graduates from the UK's most prestigious universities — including Oxford, Cambridge, LSE, Imperial, and UCL. They are independent professionals who are DBS-checked, personally interviewed, and selected based on their subject expertise, teaching experience, and communication skills.

How does payment work?

Sessions are arranged directly between families and their tutor, with payment handled securely through our platform. There is no long-term contract — you can book sessions individually or in blocks, with flexible scheduling to fit around your child's school timetable and exam dates.

Do the tutors work with students outside of the UK?

Yes. All sessions are available online via video call, so students can be supported from anywhere in the world. We have introduced tutors to families across Europe, the Middle East, and Asia who are following UK GCSE and A-Level curricula.
