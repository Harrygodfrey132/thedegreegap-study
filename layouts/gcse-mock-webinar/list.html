Meet your hosts: Joe & Harry

Joe and Harry, co-founders of The Degree Gap, have guided thousands of families through GCSEs. Their work has been featured in Forbes and The Independent, pairing high-impact academic strategy with practical support that fits real family life.

Expect a calm, confidence-building approach that turns mock season from a stress cycle into a structured plan you and your teen can follow together.

In this 45-minute masterclass, you will discover:

  • Why mocks can make or break motivation, and how to turn them into a springboard for progress rather than panic.
  • The proven RAG-rate roadmap top tutors use to channel revision time into the highest-impact actions.
  • Early signs of burnout to watch for, alongside reset routines that rebuild focus with minimal friction.
  • Conversation frameworks for keeping communication calm and collaborative through the toughest term of the year.

Every takeaway is designed to give you clarity, structure, and confidence so you can support your child without confrontation or confusion.
